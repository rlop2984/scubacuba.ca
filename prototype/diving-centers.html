Our Destinations

Two ways to dive the south coast of Cuba — choose comfort or commune with nature

We've focused our operation on two destinations along Cuba's pristine south coast — minutes apart, but worlds apart in style. Faro Luna for divers who want resort comfort and an iconic wall right off the shore. Guajimico for travelers who put nature first.

Flagship Resort
Cienfuegos · South Coast

Faro Luna

Our flagship dive operation. The reef wall starts just 60 meters from shore and drops past 100 feet — accessible by boat or directly from the dive center's steps. 30+ named sites with caves, gardens of gorgonians, and well-preserved shipwrecks.

30+ Dive Sites 5–40m Depths Shore Diving Night Dives Nitrox Resort Comfort
Plan a Faro Luna Trip
Eco-Retreat · New
Between Cienfuegos & Trinidad

Guajimico

A secluded ecotourism retreat for divers who travel light and tread softly. Low-impact bungalows tucked into the forest, untouched reefs with zero crowds, and direct access to the Escambray mountains and Nicho waterfalls. Designed for nature-first travelers.

Eco-Lodge Untouched Reefs Low-Impact Travel Forest & Mountain Nicho Waterfalls Trinidad Nearby
Plan a Guajimico Trip

What's Nearby

Trinidad

UNESCO colonial town with live music, cobblestone streets and vibrant nightlife — easy day trip from both destinations

El Nicho Waterfalls

Crystal-clear natural pools hidden in the Escambray Mountains. Especially close to Guajimico

Cienfuegos

The Pearl of the South — French-colonial architecture, seaside malecón and beautiful sunsets
