About Us

The team that makes your Cuban diving dreams come true

Our Story

Three organizations, one mission: to share Cuba's underwater paradise with divers worldwide

ScubaCuba.ca was born from a simple idea — that divers from every corner of the world deserve access to one of the Caribbean's best-kept secrets. Cuba's pristine reefs, dramatic walls, and historic shipwrecks have been waiting to be discovered, and we're here to make it happen — whether you're flying in from Toronto, New York, Madrid, Mexico City, London or Sydney.

We are the result of an exclusive alliance between three organizations, each bringing decades of expertise to create the ultimate diving vacation experience. From the moment you book to the moment you surface from your last dive, every detail is handled by professionals who live and breathe diving.

Our Partners

A powerful alliance for the perfect dive vacation

MARLIN

Cuba's official marine department under the Ministry of Tourism. They operate and maintain all 14 dive centers across the island, ensuring world-class safety standards and pristine marine environments.

Aquasub Scuba Diving Centre

Established in 2002 in Richmond Hill, Ontario. A PADI 5 Star Instructor Development Center with over 12,000 divers trained from across the Americas, Europe and beyond. Our base of dive operations on the ground in Cuba.

Hola Sun Holidays

Over 30 years in the travel industry with 1,900+ travel agency partners and access to 190 hotels in Cuba. They handle flights, transfers, and accommodations with OWG as the preferred carrier.

Norbert Pietkiewicz

Founder & Lead PADI Instructor

A former Polish Navy diver turned PADI instructor, Norbert brings over 35 years of diving experience to every trip. His passion for Cuba's underwater world led him to create ScubaCuba.ca — a company dedicated to sharing the magic of Cuban diving with adventurers from every continent.

With more than 12,000 divers trained throughout his career, Norbert's expertise, patience, and enthusiasm make every dive — whether your first or your thousandth — an unforgettable experience.

Norbert personally leads many of our group trips, ensuring that every diver receives world-class instruction and discovers the best Cuba has to offer, both above and below the water.
