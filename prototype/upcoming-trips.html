Beyond the dives

Optional excursions

When you're not diving, Cuba's culture and nature are right outside the hotel. Most divers do at least one or two of these.

🏛

Cienfuegos city tour

UNESCO heritage site, 18 km from the hotel. French colonial architecture and vibrant culture.

🏛

Trinidad city tour

UNESCO site ~70 km east. Founded in 1514, one of the best-preserved colonial towns in the Caribbean.

🌳

El Nicho protected area

~60 km away. Waterfalls, natural pools, hiking trails and zip-lining in the Escambray mountains.

🏰

Fortress of Jagua

1745 Spanish castle and museum guarding Cienfuegos bay — one of Cuba's oldest military fortifications.

🐬

Dolphinarium

Right next to the dive center. Two daily dolphin shows — perfect for non-diving days.

🦉

Guanaroca bird reservoir

~8 km away. Pink flamingos and diverse bird species in a protected lagoon.
