Optional Excursions

When you're not diving, explore Cuba's incredible culture and nature

🏛

Cienfuegos City Tour

UNESCO World Heritage site, 18km from hotel. French colonial architecture and vibrant culture.

🏛

Trinidad City Tour

UNESCO site ~70km away. Founded in 1514, one of the best-preserved colonial towns in the Caribbean.

🌳

El Nicho Protected Area

~60km from hotel. Waterfalls, natural pools, hiking trails, and zip-lining in the mountains.

🏰

Fortress of Jagua

1745 Spanish castle and museum. One of Cuba's oldest military fortifications.

🐬

Dolphinarium

Right next to the dive center. Two daily dolphin shows — perfect for non-diving days.

🦉

Guanaroca Bird Reservoir

~8km away. Home to pink flamingos and diverse bird species in a protected lagoon.
