Quote requests are free and non-binding. Pricing is confirmed in writing only after we review your specific dates and party size. A booking is confirmed only when AquaSub Travel issues a written confirmation and a deposit has been received.

Travel insurance

Travel insurance is required for all Cuba trips and is not included in the package price. We strongly recommend coverage that includes trip cancellation, medical, and emergency evacuation. Quotes for travel insurance can be added to your trip on request.

Scuba diving risks

Scuba diving carries inherent risks including decompression illness, marine-life encounters, and equipment failure. All divers must produce a valid certification card (or enroll in a course on arrival) and complete a dive medical form. Divers must follow safety briefings and the instructions of MARLIN's PADI instructors at all times.

Cancellations and changes

Cancellation and change policies depend on the type of fare booked, the airline, and the hotel. Specific terms are provided at the time of booking. In general, the closer to departure, the higher the penalty. Travel insurance with cancellation coverage is strongly recommended.
